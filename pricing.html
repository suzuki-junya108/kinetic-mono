FREQUENTLY ASKED 05 QUESTIONS
CAN I SWITCH PLANS AT ANY TIME?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing period.
WHAT HAPPENS WHEN MY FREE TRIAL ENDS?
Your 14-day free trial includes full access to all Pro features. When it ends, you can choose to subscribe to any plan. If you don't subscribe, your account will be moved to a read-only state. Your data is retained for 30 days before deletion.
DO YOU OFFER DISCOUNTS FOR STARTUPS OR NON-PROFITS?
We offer a 50% discount for verified non-profit organizations and a startup program with 12 months free on the Pro plan for companies with less than $1M in funding. Contact our sales team to learn more about eligibility requirements.
HOW IS DATA STORAGE CALCULATED?
Data storage is calculated based on the total size of your active datasets, including indexes and metadata. Archived data and backups do not count toward your storage limit. Storage usage is monitored in real-time and visible in your dashboard.
WHAT PAYMENT METHODS DO YOU ACCEPT?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers for annual plans, and wire transfers for Enterprise accounts. All payments are processed securely through Stripe. Invoicing is available for annual Enterprise plans.
